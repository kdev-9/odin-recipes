Steak

Description

Everyone has their own idea on how to cook a perfect steak. In this recipe we keep things simple by using some tried and true methods reccomended by top chefs

Ingredients

  • NY strip
  • Butter
  • Fresh garlic
  • Fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a cast iron skillet on the stove to medium-high heat
  2. Add the steak
  3. After 30 seconds, add the butter, thyme, and garlic
  4. Flip the steak after 1 minute
  5. Spoon the melted butter over the steak
  6. Cook to your preferred doneness
