Margaritas

Description

These margaritas are sure to be a hit. With only a few easy ingredients, they'll keep your guests happy and won't be much work for you.

For an extra twist, try adding a couple ounces of grapefruit or pineapple juice into the drink.

Ingredients

  • Tequila
  • Triple Sec
  • Lime Juice
  • Agave
  • Salt, for rim
